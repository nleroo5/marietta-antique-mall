📋 Summary & Recommendations

🏆 Best Choice: Option 1 (Single Column) or Option 5 (Accordion)

For Marietta Antique Mall:

  • Option 1 (Single Column) - Best if you want all info visible and easily readable. Great for older demographic who may visit antique malls.
  • Option 5 (Accordion) - Best if you want a cleaner, more modern look with progressive disclosure. Good for younger, mobile-savvy users.

✅ Good Alternative: Option 2 (Two Column Compact)

Middle ground between simplicity and space efficiency. Works well if you have moderate content and want good visual balance.

⚠️ Use With Caution: Option 3 (Asymmetric)

Only if you want to strongly emphasize contact/brand info over other content. Can feel unbalanced.

❌ Not Recommended: Option 4 (Three Column)

Too cramped for mobile. Text becomes illegibly small. Poor touch targets. Better for desktop only.

💡 My Recommendation for Your Site:

Given that your target audience likely includes older antique collectors, I'd recommend:

Option 1 (Single Column Stacked) - Maximum readability, clear hierarchy, and all info visible without interaction. Perfect for your demographic and business type.
